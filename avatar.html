What makes a home?

What makes a home Is it the people, The stuff, The place, The family, The friends? Or is it just the house you currently preside in. Or does it constitute of the place where you sleep. What is the exact definition of home, cause i cant quite pinpoint it. Even though i try thinking about it, I cant seem to form a conclusive answer. It is broad, yet slim, and not conclusive, there is no one answer. And thats hard cause what should be home, might not be. And what is, is not.

Hurt

For me. Life’s been hard, or really i’ve been tough to go along with. I have a hard time with many things, which i need to improve on. Because if i dont i might lose more people. I go too fast. And i need to improve my skills in reading my own emotions. I might need to dive back into aromanticism. And see if that is what it is. Or if there just is something wrong with me. I have obsessions of people i really really want to be friends with, and often mistake this for love, romance or sexual attraction. And then i hurt people. Because i made a mistake. Alot of people hate me, for stuff ive done on accident. And sometimes on purpose. But never with intention of hurt.

Yearning

Yearning for the past I cry out, but is it the past I need? I doubt it because, in the past, I felt the same. However, what I seem to yearn for may not even exist. It seems to stay just a fingertip away from my grasp, but I never come closer. Maybe it is the careless moments when nothing matters. Where consequences don't belong, and cannot survive. But that is not the past, that is a moment. Is it just nostalgia, that can't be it doesn't give this gripping feeling to the heart. It doesn't suffocate, it may yearn but it is not aggressive. It soothes more so.
